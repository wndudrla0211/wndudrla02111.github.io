3-Day Split Routine

This routine is designed for intermediate to advanced lifters who are looking to build muscle and strength. It includes a mix of compound and isolation exercises to target different muscle groups.

Monday - Chest & Triceps

  • Bench Press: 4 sets x 12 reps
  • Incline Dumbbell Press: 4 sets x 12 reps
  • Chest Flyes: 4 sets x 12 reps
  • Push Ups: 4 sets x 12 reps
  • Tricep Cable Pushdowns: 6 sets x 12 reps
  • Dips: 4 sets(until failure) x 12 reps

Tuesday - Back & Biceps

  • Pull-ups: 4 sets x 12 reps
  • Deadlifts: 4 sets x 8 reps
  • Barbell Rows: 4 sets x 12 reps
  • Lat Pulldowns: 4 sets x 12 reps
  • Dumbbell Rows: 4 sets x 12 reps
  • Barbell Curls: 4 sets x 12 reps
  • Hammer Curls: 4 sets x 12 reps
  • Dumbbell Curls: 4 sets x 12 reps

Wednesday - Legs & Shoulders

  • Squats: 4 sets x 12 reps
  • Leg Press: 4 sets x 12 reps
  • Leg Extensions: 4 sets x 12 reps
  • Leg Curls: 4 sets x 12 reps
  • Military Press: 4 sets x 12 reps
  • Lateral Raises: 4 sets x 12 reps
